Crystal Game

↓ INSTRUCTIONS ↓

You are given a random number at the start of the game, and it is displayed at the top left

There are four crystals below. Each one will give you a constant value that is unique to that crystal. The values given to each crystal will change every round.

The object of the game is to match your total score to the random number generated.

The values of each crystal are hidden until you click on it

Good Luck!
